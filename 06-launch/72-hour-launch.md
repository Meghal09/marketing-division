# 72‑Hour Launch Plan

This is the rapid‑deployment launch framework used to activate a new product, funnel, or campaign within 72 hours.

---

# Day 1 — Strategy + Assets

## 1. Define the Offer
- Product name
- Price
- Guarantee
- Bonuses
- CTA

## 2. Build the Funnel
- Landing page
- Checkout page
- Thank‑you page
- Email capture

## 3. Create Core Content
- 3 TikToks
- 3 Reels
- 1 LinkedIn post
- 1 Reddit value post

## 4. Set Up Email Sequence
- Welcome email
- Value email
- Offer email

- LinkedIn authority post

## 1. Launch Announcement
- TikTok launch video
- Reels launch video
- LinkedIn announcement
