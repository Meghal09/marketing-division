# Paid Ads Plan

This document outlines the paid advertising strategy for awareness, retargeting, and conversion.

---

# Campaign Types

## 1. Awareness Campaign
Purpose: Reach new audiences  
Platforms:
- TikTok Ads
- Meta Ads
- Instagram Reels Ads

Targets:
- Interests
- Lookalikes
- Broad audiences

KPIs:
- CPM
- Reach
- Engagement

---

## 2. Retargeting Campaign
Purpose: Convert warm traffic  
Targets:
- Website visitors
- Video viewers
- Email subscribers

KPIs:
- CTR
- Cost per click
- Cost per lead

---

## 3. Conversion Campaign
Purpose: Generate sales  
Targets:
- Warm audiences
- High‑intent users

KPIs:
- Cost per acquisition
- ROAS
- Conversion rate

---

# Creative Requirements
- 10 short‑form videos
- 5 static creatives
- 3 headline variations
- 3 CTA variations

---

# Optimization Framework
1. Kill ads below benchmark  
2. Scale ads above benchmark  
3. Duplicate winners  
4. Refresh creatives weekly  
5. Update hooks every 3–5 days
