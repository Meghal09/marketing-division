# Retention Funnel System

## Purpose
To retain customers, increase LTV, and reduce churn.

---

## Components
- Email nurturing  
- Community engagement  
- Upsell sequences  
- Loyalty content  

---

## Workflow
1. ATHENA‑MK defines retention strategy  
2. ECHO‑EM builds email flows  
3. HELIOS‑CM manages community  
4. CRONOS‑AN tracks retention metrics  

---

## KPIs
- Email open rate  
- Repeat purchases  
- Churn rate  
- LTV
