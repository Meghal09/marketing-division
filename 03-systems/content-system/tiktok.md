# TikTok Content System

## Purpose
The TikTok system is designed to produce high‑volume, high‑impact short‑form content that drives awareness, traffic, and conversions.

---

## Core Objectives
- Generate viral hooks
- Produce daily short‑form videos
- Maintain consistent posting schedule
- Drive traffic to funnels and offers
- Build brand authority

---

## Workflow
1. MUSE‑CG generates hooks and scripts  
2. ZEUS‑TT creates short‑form video concepts  
3. IRIS‑SM schedules and posts content  
4. CRONOS‑AN tracks performance  

---

## Content Types
- Educational micro‑lessons  
- Fast tips  
- POV storytelling  
- Before/after transformations  
- Myth‑busting  
- Reaction‑style commentary  

---

## KPIs
- Views  
- Watch time  
- Shares  
- Profile visits  
- Link clicks
