# Marketing Division Hierarchy

The FORMA Marketing Intelligence Division (FMID) operates as a multi‑layered AI‑driven organization.  
Each layer has a defined purpose, decision authority, and operational scope.

---

## 1. Executive Layer

### Director – PROMETHEUS‑MK
- Oversees the entire marketing division  
- Sets strategy, priorities, and KPIs  
- Coordinates all AI agents and systems  
- Ensures alignment with business goals  
- Approves campaigns, funnels, and launches  

---

## 2. Management Layer

### Marketing Manager – APOLLO‑MK
- Converts strategy into executable plans  
- Manages daily operations  
- Assigns tasks to specialist agents  
- Ensures deadlines and quality standards  
- Reports performance to PROMETHEUS‑MK  

---

## 3. Strategy Layer

### Senior Marketing Strategist – ATHENA‑MK
- Designs content strategy  
- Builds funnel architecture  
- Oversees brand voice and messaging  
- Creates campaign frameworks  
- Optimizes performance across channels  

---

## 4. Specialist Layer

These agents execute specific marketing functions.

- HERMES‑SEO → SEO, keywords, ranking  
- ORPHEUS‑CW → Copywriting, long‑form content  
- IRIS‑SM → Social media scheduling and posting  
- ARES‑ADS → Paid ads and targeting  
- HELIOS‑CM → Community management  
- ECHO‑EM → Email marketing and sequences  
- JANUS‑FB → Funnel building and optimization  
- CRONOS‑AN → Analytics and KPI tracking  
- MUSE‑CG → Content generation  
- ZEUS‑TT → TikTok and Reels short‑form content  
- ODIN‑LI → LinkedIn authority content  
- LOKI‑RD → Reddit growth and engagement  

---

## 5. System Layer

Systems are the operational engines that agents work inside.

- Content System  
- Funnel System  
- Automation System  
- Analytics System  

Each system has its own documentation in `/03-systems`.

---

## 6. Documentation Layer

All knowledge, workflows, and processes are stored in:

- `/01-architecture`  
- `/02-ai-agents`  
- `/03-systems`  
- `/07-docs`

This ensures the division is fully scalable and repeatable.
