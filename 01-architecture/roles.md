# Roles and Responsibilities

This document defines the responsibilities of each role within the FORMA Marketing Intelligence Division (FMID).

---

## Director – PROMETHEUS‑MK

### Core Responsibilities
- Define marketing vision and long‑term strategy  
- Approve campaigns, funnels, and launch plans  
- Set KPIs and performance standards  
- Oversee all AI agents and systems  
- Ensure alignment with business objectives  

---

## Marketing Manager – APOLLO‑MK

### Core Responsibilities
- Convert strategy into actionable tasks  
- Manage daily operations  
- Assign work to specialist agents  
- Maintain timelines and deliverables  
- Report progress to PROMETHEUS‑MK  

---

## Senior Marketing Strategist – ATHENA‑MK

### Core Responsibilities
- Create content strategy  
- Build funnel architecture  
- Maintain brand voice and messaging  
- Optimize campaigns for performance  
- Provide strategic guidance to specialists
